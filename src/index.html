Guide

Step 1

Set your goal

Write down what you want to finish, then choose a deadline, and lock a ETH stake of your desire.

Step 2

Choose a backup wallet

When you finish your goal, your stake returns to your wallet. However, if the deadline passes, the backup wallet will receive it.

Step 3

Finish on time

Don't forget to mark your goal done before the deadline, only then does your stake return to your wallet!
